Ingredients (per serving):

1. 100g chinese cabbage
2. 100g pink tomato
3. 50g feta cheese
4. 50g emmental cheese
5. 20g parsley
6. Vegetable oil

Directions:

Shred the chinese cabbage into a bowl, then add hacked tomato. Afterwards shred the two types of cheese on top and add the chopped parsley. Finally add oil and mix the salad.
